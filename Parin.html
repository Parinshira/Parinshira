    WE'RE LEADER IN OIL MARKET

    Following the mantra, "Quality always matters" Mr. Ladani Param founded Astu Groundnut in the year 2021. Thanks to our commitment to quality, we have emerged as one of the most reputed brand in the oil industry today; manufacturing and supplying best quality of groundnut oil. Our gamut of products consists of groundnut, soybean, sesame, mustard, black and green gram, sesame, cumin, coriander, mustard, bajra, wheat, corn, etc.

    Our mammoth production unit is located on Veraval Highway near keshod. It is spread across 13000 sq. ft. area and boasts of the most advanced machinery and technology which equip us to process the oil hygienically in the most efficient manner.
